Accessibility Statement

Accessibility was prioritized from the beginning of the design and development process. I aligned the site with WCAG 2.0 and the POUR principles (Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, Robust). All images include descriptive alt text, navigation elements are clearly labeled, and colour contrast ratios were tested to meet standards.
